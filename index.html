    • Welcome...

    Sterling farm Research & Services Pvt. Ltd. an Industry entity committed to the development of agriculture by way of producing various types of Eco Friendly Agricultural Inputs. Sterling Group of Companies was established in 1987 by professionals from Life Science business with a commitment to support farming community by Researching, developing, manufacturing and introducing high tech agri-inputs for improving productivity without harming the environment.

    There is money in Agriculture and it should be considered as an industry. Our people are our strength. We have trained professionals in our organization.We are in the process of developing various agriculture educational kits or children so as to make them understand the importance of agriculture from and early age
